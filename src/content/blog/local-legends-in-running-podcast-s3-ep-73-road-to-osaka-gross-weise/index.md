The [Local Legends in Running Podcast](https://open.spotify.com/show/5Fo6hoM27djoiyyky4amKl?si=30236ca701d14c8a) Road to Osaka Series documents the marathon training build of Clive Gross, [Bush Turkey Track Club](http://www.bushturkey.club) runner of Brisbane, and Kyle Weise, coach of [Kyles Run Coaching](https://www.instagram.com/kylesruncoaching/) on the Gold Coast. They detail their training weeks along with discussing important marathon topics such as nutrition, mental applications, race strategies and footwear.
